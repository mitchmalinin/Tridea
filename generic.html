About Us

The Big Picture


It all started when we realized a major problem in the programing world...lack of communication between developers, artists, and brilliant idea carriers. So, we decided to join together to think of a way to bring these talented people together to make AMAZING things. We traveled far and wide, searching for the answer to this dilemma until one fateful night we all decided to go get ice cream at our favorite local ice cream shop. It was at this shop that we figured it out, TRIDEA. We decided to make a website that would bring those with ideas to work with programmers and artists to make ideas come to LIFE, and make dreams come true. We are proud to present the newest and simplest way to do freelance and we are proud to call it Tridea, may your wildest ideas become a reality.
